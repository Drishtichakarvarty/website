Seviyan Kheer(Vermicelli Kheer)

This creamy seviyan kheer is a North Indian sweet made with wheat vermicelli, milk, sugar, dry fruits and flavored with spices

Milk Cake

Indian Milk Cake is a traditional Indian milk fudge prepared with full cream milk, sugar, ghee, and a coagulant!

Gulab Jamun

Gulab jamun is a sweet confectionary or dessert, originating in the Indian subcontinent and a type of mithai popular in India,the Maldives (where it is known as gulab ki janu).

Gajar Ka Halwa

Gajar ka halwa is a carrot-based sweet dessert pudding made by placing grated carrots in a pot containing a specific amount of water, milk and sugar, cardamom and then cooking while stirring regularly. It is often served with a garnish of almonds and pistachios

Cakes

Cake is a flour confection made from flour, sugar, and other ingredients, and is usually baked.

Kaju Katli

Kaju katli, also known as kaju barfi, is an Indian dessert similar to a barfi. Kaju means cashew; barfi is often made by thickening milk with sugar and other ingredients
